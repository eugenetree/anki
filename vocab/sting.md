# Flash Card: Sting

## Front

Коли я ріжу цибулю, вона завжди <u>пече в очах</u>.
Після плавання в хлорованому басейні у мене <u>пекло в очах</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "When I cut onions, they always sting in my eyes. After swimming in a chlorinated pool, my eyes were stinging."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: sting (verb), stinging (present participle)<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to cause a sharp, burning pain or uncomfortable sensation, often from chemicals, smoke, or irritants<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The soap got in my eyes and started stinging.<br />
  Scrub with all the chemicals and stinging in your eyes.<br />
  The smoke from the fire was stinging my throat.<br />
  My skin is still stinging from the sunburn.
</div>
