# Flash Card: Strand

## Front

Ми <u>застрягли</u> в аеропорту через скасований рейс і не могли дістатися додому. (not "stuck")
Буря <u>залишила</u> тисячі людей на вокзалі без можливості виїхати. (not "left")

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "We were stranded at the airport due to a cancelled flight and couldn't get home. The storm stranded thousands of travelers at the train station with no way to leave."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: strand / be stranded<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to leave someone in a place or situation without any way to leave or get help. Most commonly used in passive form "be stranded" (застрягти, опинитися в скрутній ситуації)<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The storm stranded thousands of travelers at the train station.<br />
  When my car broke down, I was stranded on the highway for hours.<br />
  The tourists were stranded on the island when the ferry stopped running.
</div>
