# Flash Card: Cook Up

## Front

Таке неможливо <u>придумати</u> самотужки.
Вона швидко <u>вигадала</u> пояснення, чому запізнилася.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "You can't cook this up on your own. She quickly cooked up an explanation for why she was late."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: cook up<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to invent, devise, or put together an idea, plan, story, or explanation, often informally or with some creativity; make up is broader and often just means to invent something, especially something false, while cook up can sound more like creating a plan or story with effort or a slightly suspicious feeling<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  You can't cook up a story like that without help.<br />
  They cooked up a plan to surprise their friend after work.<br />
  He cooked up an excuse for missing the meeting.
</div>
