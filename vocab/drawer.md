# Flash Card: Drawer

## Front

Я поклала ключі у верхню <u>шухляду</u> біля дверей.
У письмовому столі застрягла нижня <u>шухляда</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "I put the keys in the top drawer by the door. The bottom drawer in the desk got stuck."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: drawer<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: a box-shaped part of a piece of furniture that slides in and out and is used for storing things<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  She opened the kitchen drawer and took out a spoon.<br />
  I keep my passport in the top drawer of my desk.<br />
  The drawer was full of old receipts and batteries.
