# Flash Card: rib

## Front (Ukrainian)

У мене болить <u>ребро</u> після падіння з велосипеда.

## Back (English)

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "My rib hurts after falling off the bicycle."<br><br>
  <strong>Word</strong>: rib<br><br>
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: one of the curved bones that form the chest wall and protect the lungs and heart (in humans and animals)<br><br>
   <strong>Examples</strong>:<br>
   He broke a rib in the car accident.<br>
   The doctor examined her ribs to check for any fractures.<br>
   She felt a sharp pain in her rib when she coughed.
</div>
