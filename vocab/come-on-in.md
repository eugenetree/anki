# Come On In

## Front

Двері відчинені, <u>заходь до середини</u>!

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The door is open, come on in!"<br /><br />
  <strong>Phrase</strong>: come on in<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: an informal, friendly way to invite someone to
  enter a place<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Come on in, make yourself at home.<br />
  Don't stand outside, come on in and have some tea.<br />
  When I knocked, she opened the door and said, "Come on in!"
</div>
