# Flash Card: I could so use

## Front

Після безсонної ночі мені б зараз <u>так не завадила</u> чашка кави.
Після такого важкого дня мені б так не завадив хороший масаж.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "After a sleepless night, I could so use a cup of coffee right now. After such a hard day, I could so use a good massage."<br /><br />
  <strong>Phrase</strong>: I could so use (informal emphasis)<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: An informal way to emphasize that you really need or want something. "So" is used as an intensifier between "could" and the verb to add emphasis.<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I could so use a vacation right now after all this stress.<br />
  After working all night, I could so use a cup of coffee.<br />
  I could so use some help with this project.<br />
  It's freezing outside – I could so use a warm blanket.
</div>
