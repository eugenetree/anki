# Flash Card: The Bottom of the Barrel

## Front

Після того, як всі хороші кандидати відмовилися, нам довелося вибирати з <u>найгіршого, що залишилося</u>.
Ці товари - це справді <u>найгірше, що є</u>, але іншого вибору немає.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "After all the good candidates refused, we had to choose from the bottom of the barrel. These goods are really the bottom of the barrel, but there's no other choice."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: the bottom of the barrel<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: the worst quality or the last remaining options when everything else has been used or exhausted<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  We've hired everyone else, so now we're scraping the bottom of the barrel.<br />
  The store was closing, so they were selling the bottom of the barrel items at huge discounts.<br />
  After all the good players were drafted, the team had to pick from the bottom of the barrel.
</div>
