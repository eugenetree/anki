# Flash Card: Work Out

## Front

Не хвилюйся, все <u>вийде</u> добре.
Немає нічого, що ми не можемо <u>вирішити</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "Don't worry, everything will work out." / "There is nothing we can't work out."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: work out<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: when a situation or plan develops successfully or turns out well; to have a positive result or outcome<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Things didn't work out between them.<br />
  I hope this plan works out.<br />
  Everything worked out in the end.<br />
  If things don't work out, we'll try something else.<br />
</div>
