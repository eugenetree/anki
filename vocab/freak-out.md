# Flash Card: Freak Out

## Front

Не <u>панікуй</u>, все буде добре.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "Don't freak out, everything will be fine."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: freak out<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to become very anxious, scared, angry, or upset; to lose control emotionally or panic; can also mean to cause someone to become scared or upset (transitive use)<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  She freaked out when she saw the spider in her room.<br />
  My parents will freak out if I come home late.<br />
  The loud noise freaked out the dog.<br />
  I totally freaked out when I couldn't find my wallet.
</div>
