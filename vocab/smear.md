# Flash Card: Smear

## Front

Політик сказав, що опоненти намагаються <u>очорнити</u> його перед виборами.
Багато людей сприйняли цю статтю як <u>наклеп</u> на мера.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The politician said his opponents were trying to smear him before the election. Many people saw the article as a smear against the mayor."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: smear<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to unfairly damage someone's reputation by spreading negative, often false or misleading information; as a noun, a smear is an unfair attack on someone's reputation<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The article tried to smear the candidate with old rumors.<br />
  She called the accusation a deliberate smear.<br />
  The company denied the claims and said they were part of a smear campaign.
</div>
