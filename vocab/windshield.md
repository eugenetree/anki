# Flash Card: Windshield

## Front

На <u>лобовому склі</u> з'явилася тріщина після того, як камінь вдарив по ньому.
Дощ покрив <u>лобове скло</u>, тому я включив склоочисники.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "A crack appeared on the windshield after a stone hit it. The rain covered the windshield, so I turned on the windshield wipers."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: windshield<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: the front window of a vehicle, typically made of glass or plastic, that protects the driver and passengers from wind, rain, and debris<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The windshield was covered in frost this morning.<br />
  A bird hit the windshield while we were driving on the highway.<br />
  He had to replace the windshield after the accident.
</div>
