# Flash Card: To Approach

## Front

Коли ми <u>наближалися</u> до міста, пейзаж став більш урбанізованим.
Як ти <u>підходиш</u> до вирішення цієї проблеми?
Він наважився <u>підійти</u> до неї і попросити про допомогу.
Вони <u>наближаються</u> до твоєї машини.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "As we approached the city, the landscape became more urbanized. How do you approach solving this problem? He dared to approach her and ask for help. They're approaching your car."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: to approach<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: (1) to move closer to something or someone physically; (2) to deal with or handle a problem, situation, or task in a particular way; (3) to speak to someone, especially to ask for something or make a request<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The train is approaching the station slowly.<br />
  They're approaching your car.<br />
  We need to approach this issue from a different angle.<br />
  She approached her boss about getting a raise.<br />
  The deadline is approaching, so we need to work faster.<br />
  He approached the problem systematically and found a solution.
</div>
