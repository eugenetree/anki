# Flash Card: Pitch in

## Front

Якщо всі <u>долучаться</u>, ми закінчимо роботу швидше.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "If everyone pitches in, we'll finish the work faster."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: pitch in<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to help or contribute to something, especially by doing your share of work or giving money; to join others in an effort or task<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Everyone pitched in to clean up after the party.<br />
  Can you pitch in a few dollars for the gift?<br />
  We all pitched in to help move the furniture.<br />
  If we all pitch in, we can finish this project on time.
</div>
