# Flash Card: to duck

## Front (Ukrainian)

Він постійно <u>ухиляється</u> від відповідальності за свої помилки.
Ти не зможеш мене постійно <u>уникати</u>.

## Back (English)

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He constantly ducks responsibility for his mistakes."<br />
  "You won't be able to constantly avoid me."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: to duck<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to avoid or evade something, especially a question, responsibility, or duty<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The politician tried to duck the reporter's difficult question.<br />
  Don't duck your responsibilities at work.<br />
  She always ducks when someone asks her about her future plans.
</div>
