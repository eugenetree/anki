# Flash Card: Come Out (Express Position)

## Front

Джессі, яку позицію ти <u>займаєш</u> щодо всього цього?
Де ти <u>стоїш</u> у питанні нової податкової політики?

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>:<br />
  "Jessy, where do you come out on all this?"<br />
  "Where do you come out on the new tax policy?"<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: come out (express one's position/opinion)<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to state or reveal your opinion, stance, or position on a matter or issue<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Where do you come out on the new tax policy?<br />
  After all the discussion, he came out in favor of the proposal.<br />
  The CEO came out strongly against the merger.<br />
  I'm still thinking about it, but I'll let you know where I come out.
</div>
