# Flash Card: Anticipate

## Front

Ми <u>очікуємо</u> збільшення продажів наступного місяця.
Вони <u>передбачили</u> всі можливі проблеми заздалегідь.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "We anticipate an increase in sales next month. They anticipated all possible problems in advance."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: anticipate<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to expect something to happen and prepare for it; to think about and deal with something before it happens<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I anticipate some difficulties with this project.<br />
  The company anticipates strong growth this year.<br />
  We didn't anticipate such a large turnout.<br />
  She anticipated his reaction and prepared her response.
</div>
