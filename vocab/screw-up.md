# Flash Card: Screw Up

## Front

Я <u>облажався</u> на іспиті, бо не вивчив матеріал.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "I screwed up on the exam because I didn't study the material."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: screw up<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to make a mistake or do something badly; to ruin or mess something up (informal)<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I really screwed up that presentation at work today.<br />
  Don't screw up this opportunity, it's your last chance.<br />
  He screwed up the recipe by adding too much salt.
