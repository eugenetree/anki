# Flash Card: Your guess is as good as mine

## Front

- Коли почнеться зустріч? <u>- Я не знаю більше за тебе</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "When will the meeting start? Your guess is as good as mine."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: Your guess is as good as mine<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: an informal way to say "I don't know either" or "I have no more information than you do"; used when you don't know the answer to a question<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Where did he go? Your guess is as good as mine.<br />
  What's causing the problem? Your guess is as good as mine.<br />
  When will they arrive? Your guess is as good as mine.<br />
  Why did she quit? Your guess is as good as mine.
</div>
