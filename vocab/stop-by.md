# Flash Card: Stop By

## Front

Я <u>заскочу</u> сьогодні ввечері на кілька хвилин.
Чому б тобі не <u>завітати</u> до нас на каву якось?

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "I'll stop by tonight for a few minutes. Why don't you stop by our place for coffee sometime?"<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: stop by<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to visit someone or a place briefly and casually, usually without staying long or without planning it in advance<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I'll stop by later tonight if you're free.<br />
  Feel free to stop by anytime you're in the neighborhood.<br />
  Can you stop by my office later to pick up those documents?
</div>
