# Flash Card: Turn Up

## Front

Він нарешті <u>з'явився</u> на вечірці о 10 годині вечора.
Мої загублені ключі <u>знайшлися</u> в шухляді.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He finally turned up at the party at 10 PM. My lost keys turned up in the drawer."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: turn up<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: (1) to arrive or appear, often unexpectedly or after being awaited; (2) to be found or discovered, especially after being lost or missing<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  She didn't turn up for the meeting yesterday.<br />
  I thought he wouldn't turn up, but he showed up on time.<br />
  The missing documents turned up in an old filing cabinet.<br />
  My wallet turned up under the couch after I looked everywhere.
</div>
