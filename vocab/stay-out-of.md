# Flash Card: Stay out of

## Front

Він сказав мені <u>триматися подалі</u> від його справ.
<u>Тримайся</u> подалі від моєї території.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He told me to stay out of his business."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: stay out of<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to avoid entering a place or becoming involved in a situation; to keep away from something<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Stay out of my territory.<br />
  You should stay out of other people's arguments.<br />
  The sign warned us to stay out of the construction area.<br />
  I try to stay out of office politics.
</div>
