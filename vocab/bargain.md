# Flash Card: Bargain

## Front

Я купив цей светр за половину ціни – це справжня <u>вигідна покупка</u>!

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "I bought this sweater for half price – it's a real bargain!"<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: bargain<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: something bought for less than the usual price; a good deal<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  These shoes were a real bargain at only $20.<br />
  She loves hunting for bargains at thrift stores.<br />
  At this price, the laptop is an absolute bargain.
</div>
