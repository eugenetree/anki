# Flash Card: Occasionally

## Front

Я <u>інколи</u> відвідую батьків, бо вони живуть далеко. (not sometimes)
Вона <u>час від часу</u> заходить до кав'ярні після роботи. (not from time to time)

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "I occasionally visit my parents because they live far away. She occasionally stops by the café after work."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: occasionally<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: sometimes but not often; from time to time; at infrequent or irregular intervals<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I occasionally go to the gym on weekends.<br />
  He occasionally forgets to lock the door when he leaves.<br />
  We occasionally meet for coffee to catch up.<br />
  The weather is occasionally unpredictable in this region.
</div>
