# Flash Card: Substantial

## Front

Компанія зробила <u>значний</u> прогрес у цьому році.
Він отримав <u>значну</u> суму грошей від інвесторів.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The company made substantial progress this year." / "He received a substantial amount of money from investors."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: substantial<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: large in amount, size, or importance; considerable<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The project requires a substantial investment of time and money.<br />
  There has been substantial improvement in her English skills.<br />
  The company reported substantial profits this quarter.<br />
  We need substantial evidence to support this claim.
</div>
