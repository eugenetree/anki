# Humiliate

## Front

Вчителька публічно <u>принизила</u> учня перед усім класом.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The teacher publicly humiliated the student in front of the whole class."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: humiliate<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to make someone feel ashamed or foolish, especially by hurting their dignity or self-respect<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The boss humiliated his employee by criticizing him in front of everyone.<br />
  She felt humiliated when her mistake was pointed out publicly.<br />
  Don't humiliate others just to make yourself feel better.
</div>
