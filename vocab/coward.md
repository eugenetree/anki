# Flash Card: Coward

## Front

Він втік і залишив друзів самих, бо виявився <u>боягузом</u>.
Не називай його <u>боягузом</u> лише тому, що він не хоче ризикувати життям.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He ran away and left his friends alone because he turned out to be a coward. Don't call him a coward just because he doesn't want to risk his life."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: coward<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: a person who is not brave enough to do something dangerous, difficult, or morally right<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  He felt like a coward for not speaking up.<br />
  Only a coward would blame someone else for his own mistake.<br />
  She refused to be called a coward for walking away from the fight.
</div>
