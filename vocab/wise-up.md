# Flash Card: wise up

## Front (Ukrainian)

Ти маєш <u>одуматись</u> і не робити цю помилку.

## Back (English)

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "You need to wise up and not make this mistake."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: wise up<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to think better of something; to reconsider and change your mind about a decision<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  You should wise up and not quit your job without having another one.<br />
  I wised up and decided not to buy that expensive car.<br />
  He wised up at the last minute and didn't send that angry email.
</div>
