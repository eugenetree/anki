# Flash Card: sneak

## Front (Ukrainian)

Діти намагалися <u>прокрастися</u> на кухню, щоб взяти цукерки.
Він завжди <u>крадеться</u> по коридору, щоб ніхто його не почув.

## Back (English)

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The children tried to sneak into the kitchen to get candy."<br />
  "He always sneaks down the hallway so no one hears him."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: sneak<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to move quietly and secretly, often to avoid being seen or heard<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The cat tried to sneak past the sleeping dog.<br />
  She sneaked out of the house without waking her parents.<br />
  The thief sneaked into the building through the back door.
</div>
