## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The teacher got sick, so we had a substitute come to us." / "I substituted sugar with honey in this recipe."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: substitute<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: 1) (noun) a person or thing that takes the place of another; 2) (verb) to replace someone or something with another person or thing<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  We had a substitute teacher today because Mr. Smith was absent.<br />
  You can substitute olive oil for butter in most recipes.<br />
  There's no substitute for hard work and dedication.<br />
  The coach decided to substitute the injured player in the second half.
</div>
