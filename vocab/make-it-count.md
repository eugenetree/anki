# Flash Card: Make It Count

## Front

У нас тільки один вікенд у Римі, тож давайте <u>використаємо його по максимуму</u>.
Ти готувався до цього співбесіди місяцями — <u>використай шанс на повну</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "We only have one weekend in Rome, so let's make it count. 'You've been preparing for this interview for months — make it count.'"<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: make it count<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to make something meaningful, valuable, or worthwhile; to use an opportunity effectively so it has a positive impact<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  You only have one chance, so make it count.<br />
  She decided to make every day count by helping others.<br />
  This is your opportunity to prove yourself - make it count.<br />
  He wanted to make his time at university count by studying hard.
</div>
