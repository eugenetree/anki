# Flash Card: Swing By

## Front

Я можу <u>заскочити</u> до тебе після роботи.
Можемо <u>заскочити</u> в магазин по дорозі додому.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "I can swing by your place after work. We can swing by the store on the way home."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: swing by<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to visit someone or somewhere briefly, usually on the way to another destination<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I'll swing by your office later to pick up the documents.<br />
  Can you swing by the grocery store on your way home?<br />
  She said she would swing by around 3 PM to drop off the package.<br />
  We should swing by the bank before heading to the restaurant.
</div>
