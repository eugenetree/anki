# Flash Card: Light Up

## Front

Він вийшов на вулицю, щоб <u>закурити</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He went outside to light up."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: light up<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: (informal) to light and start smoking a cigarette<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  He stepped outside to light up a cigarette.<br />
  She never lights up indoors anymore.<br />
  After the meal, several people went outside to light up.
