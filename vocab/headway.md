# Flash Card: Headway

## Front

Ми нарешті почали робити <u>прогрес</u> у вирішенні цієї проблеми.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "We finally started to make headway in solving this problem."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: headway (make headway)<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: progress or advancement, especially when you're moving forward after facing difficulties or obstacles<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The team is making headway on the new project despite the challenges.<br />
  It took months, but we're finally making some headway with the negotiations.<br />
  The scientists made significant headway in finding a cure for the disease.
</div>
