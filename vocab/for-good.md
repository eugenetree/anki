# Flash Card: For Good

## Front

Він пішов <u>назавжди</u> і більше не повернеться.
Я вирішив кинути палити <u>назавжди</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He left for good and won't come back. I decided to quit smoking for good."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: for good<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: permanently; forever; with no intention of returning or changing back<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  She moved to London for good last year.<br />
  After the argument, he left the company for good.<br />
  I've decided to stop drinking coffee for good.
</div>
