# Flash Card: To Be Up To

## Front

Чим ти зараз <u>займаєшся</u>? (not doing)
Чим ти <u>займаєшся</u> сьогодні?

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "What are you up to right now? What are you up to today?"<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: (to be) up to<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: an informal way to ask what someone is doing or what activities they are engaged in at the moment<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  What are you up to today?<br />
  I'm not up to anything at the moment, just relaxing.<br />
  Hey, what have you been up to lately?
</div>
