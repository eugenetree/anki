# Flash Card: Order

## Front

Капітан віддав <u>наказ</u> відступити від позиції.
Він слідував <u>наказу</u> без запитань.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The captain gave the order to retreat from the position. He followed the order without questions."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: order<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: a command or instruction that must be followed, especially one given by someone in authority<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The general issued an order to advance.<br />
  She received strict orders not to leave the building.<br />
  He followed his boss's orders without hesitation.<br />
  The police officer gave the order to stop.
</div>
