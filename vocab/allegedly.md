# Flash Card: Allegedly

## Front

Він <u>нібито</u> вкрав гроші з банку, але докази ще не знайдені.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He allegedly stole money from the bank, but the evidence hasn't been found yet."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: allegedly<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: used to convey that something is claimed or stated to be true, but it has not been proven or confirmed<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The politician allegedly accepted bribes from several companies.<br />
  She allegedly saw the suspect leaving the building at midnight.<br />
  The company allegedly violated environmental regulations.<br />
  He was allegedly involved in the fraud scheme.
</div>
