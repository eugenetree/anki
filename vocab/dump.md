# Flash Card: Dump

## Front

Вона <u>покинула</u> його через телефон, не пояснивши причини.
Він <u>покинув</u> свою дівчину після трьох років стосунків.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "She dumped him over the phone without explaining why. He dumped his girlfriend after three years of relationship."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: dump<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to end a romantic relationship with someone, especially suddenly or unkindly<br /><br />
  <strong>Synonyms</strong>: break up with, leave, abandon, ditch<br /><br />
  <strong>Difference</strong>: "Dump" is informal and often implies a sudden, unkind, or one-sided ending. "Break up with" is more neutral and formal. "Leave" is more general and can be less harsh. "Abandon" is more dramatic and implies leaving someone in a difficult situation. "Ditch" is very informal, similar to "dump" but even more casual.<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  He dumped her right before their anniversary.<br />
  She was devastated when her boyfriend dumped her via text message.<br />
  After months of fighting, they decided it was time to dump each other.
</div>
