# Flash Card: Strap

## Front

<u>Пристебніться</u>, будь ласка, перед зльотом.
<u>Прикріпи</u> багаж до даху машини.
Вона <u>пристебнула</u> шолом перед їздою на велосипеді.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "Strap yourself in, please, before takeoff. Strap the luggage on(to) the car roof. She strapped on her helmet before riding the bike."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: strap (verb)<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to fasten or secure someone or something with a strap or straps; to bind or attach using a band of material. Use "strap IN" when securing yourself/someone into a seat or vehicle (inside something). Use "strap ON" when attaching something to yourself or another object (onto something).<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Strap in before the plane takes off.<br />
  Make sure you strap the luggage on the roof of the car.<br />
  He strapped the baby into the car seat before driving.<br />
  She strapped on her helmet before riding the bike.
</div>
