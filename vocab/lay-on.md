# Flash Card: Lay Something On Someone

## Front

Не <u>звинувачуй мене</u> в цьому, це не моя провина. (not blame)
Він завжди <u>перекладає свої проблеми</u> на інших.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "Don't lay that on me, it's not my fault. He always lays his problems on others."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: lay something on someone<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to tell someone something burdensome or unwanted; to put blame, responsibility, or a problem on someone; to impose a burden on someone<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Don't lay that guilt trip on me.<br />
  She laid all the blame on her coworker.<br />
  Stop laying your stress on me, I have my own problems.<br />
  He's always laying his responsibilities on others.
</div>
