# Flash Card: You're up

## Front

Гаразд, команда, хто наступний? Джон, <u>твоя черга</u>!

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "Okay, team, who's next? John, you're up!"<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: you're up<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: it's your turn to do something; you are next in line for an activity, task, or game<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  You're up next to present your project.<br />
  The coach said you're up to bat.<br />
  When Sarah finishes, you're up.<br />
  You're up! Go ahead and take your turn.
</div>
