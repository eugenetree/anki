# Flash Card: Appealing

## Front

Ця пропозиція звучить дуже <u>привабливо</u> для мене. (not attractive)

## Back

<div id="back">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "This offer sounds very appealing to me."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: appealing<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: attractive or interesting; pleasing and likely to create interest or desire. While "attractive" is more commonly associated with physical appearance or visual qualities, "appealing" is more naturally used for ideas, offers, proposals, and options, emphasizing that something creates interest or desire.<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The idea of working from home is very appealing to many people.<br />
  The restaurant's menu looks appealing with so many delicious options.<br />
  She found his honesty and kindness particularly appealing.
</div>
