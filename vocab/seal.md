# Flash Card: Seal

## Front

Рукостискання <u>закріпило</u> нашу домовленість.
Тепер час поговорити про <u>укладення</u> угоди.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The handshake sealed our agreement. Now it's time to talk about sealing the deal."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: seal (to finalize/complete)<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to finalize, complete, or make something official and certain<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Now it's time to talk about sealing the deal.<br />
  After weeks of negotiation, they finally sealed the deal.<br />
  We sealed the contract with a signature yesterday.<br />
  His amazing performance sealed the victory for our team.<br />
  That decision sealed her fate in the company.
</div>
