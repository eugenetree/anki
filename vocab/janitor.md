# Flash Card: janitor

## Front (Ukrainian)

<u>Двірник</u> щодня прибирає коридори в нашій школі.

## Back (English)

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The janitor cleans the hallways in our school every day."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: janitor<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: a person whose job is to clean and maintain buildings, especially schools, offices, or apartment buildings<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The janitor fixed the broken door in the classroom.<br />
  Our school janitor is very friendly and always says hello.<br />
  The janitor mops the floors every evening after everyone leaves.
</div>
