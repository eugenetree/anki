# Flash Card: Break Down

## Front

Дозволь мені <u>детально пояснити</u> тобі всі витрати на цей проект.
Тепер, перш ніж продовжити, давайте це детально <u>розберемо</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "Let me break down all the costs for this project for you. Now before we continue let's break this down."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: break down<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to divide something into smaller parts or to explain something in detail by separating it into components to make it easier to understand<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Can you break down the budget for me?<br />
  The instructor broke down the dance routine step by step.<br />
  Let me break down how the system works.<br />
  The report breaks down sales figures by region.
</div>
