## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "His explanation seems quite plausible." / "This theory sounds quite plausible."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: plausible<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: seeming reasonable or probable; appearing to be true or believable even if not proven<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Her story was plausible, so we believed her.<br />
  The scientist offered a plausible explanation for the phenomenon.<br />
  It's plausible that he forgot about the meeting since he's been so busy.
</div>
