# Flash Card: Germ

## Front

Обов'язково мий руки, щоб не поширювати <u>мікроби</u>.
Лікар сказав, що <u>бактерії</u> можуть викликати інфекцію.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "Make sure to wash your hands to avoid spreading germs. The doctor said that germs can cause infection."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: germ<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: a microorganism, especially one that causes disease<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The doctor explained that germs can spread through contact.<br />
  Make sure to disinfect surfaces to kill any germs.<br />
  Children should learn to wash their hands to avoid germs.<br />
  The flu is caused by germs that spread from person to person.
</div>
