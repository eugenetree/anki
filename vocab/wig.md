# Flash Card: Wig

## Front

Вона одягла <u>перуку</u> для театральної вистави.
Актор носив білу <u>перуку</u>, щоб виглядати як старий чоловік.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "She put on a wig for the theater performance. The actor wore a white wig to look like an old man."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: wig<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: a covering for the head made of real or artificial hair, worn to conceal baldness or as part of a costume<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  She bought a blonde wig for the costume party.<br />
  The judge wore a traditional white wig in court.<br />
  He lost his hair during chemotherapy and started wearing a wig.
</div>
