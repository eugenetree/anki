# Flash Card: Piss off

## Front

Ти мене справді <u>бісиш</u> своїми постійними скаргами.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "You're really pissing me off with your constant complaints."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: piss off (informal/vulgar)<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to make someone annoyed or angry (informal and somewhat vulgar expression)<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Stop doing that, you're pissing me off!<br />
  His arrogant attitude really pisses me off.<br />
  It pisses me off when people are late without calling.<br />
  That noise is starting to piss everyone off.
</div>
