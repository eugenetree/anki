# Flash Card: Skim

## Front

Я маю <u>швидко переглянути</u> цей документ перед зустріччю.
Він <u>пробіг очима</u> по статті, щоб знайти основну інформацію.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "I need to skim this document before the meeting. He skimmed through the article to find the main information."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: skim<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to read quickly through something to get the main ideas or find specific information without reading every word. Unlike "look through" (which can be done at any pace), "skim" specifically emphasizes speed and a quick, superficial reading<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I only had time to skim the report before the presentation.<br />
  She skimmed the newspaper headlines while drinking her coffee.<br />
  He skimmed through the book to find the relevant chapter.<br />
  Can you skim this email and tell me if it's urgent?
</div>
