# Flash Card: Greedy

## Front

Він дуже <u>жадібний</u> до грошей і ніколи не ділиться з іншими.
Вона завжди хоче більше, вона така <u>жадібна</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He is very greedy for money and never shares with others. She always wants more, she is so greedy."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: greedy<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: having or showing an intense and selfish desire for something, especially wealth, food, or possessions<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The greedy businessman took all the profits for himself.<br />
  Don't be greedy - leave some cake for others.<br />
  He was greedy for power and would do anything to get it.<br />
  The greedy child grabbed all the toys and wouldn't share.
</div>
