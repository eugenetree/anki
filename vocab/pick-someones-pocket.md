# Flash Card: Pick Someone's Pocket

## Front

Хтось <u>обікрав мою кишеню</u> в переповненому автобусі.
Він намагався <u>витягнути гаманець з кишені</u> старого чоловіка на ринку.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "Someone picked my pocket on the crowded bus. He tried to pick the old man's pocket at the market."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: pick someone's pocket<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to steal from someone's pocket, especially by stealthily taking something (like a wallet or phone) without them noticing<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Be careful in crowded places - thieves might try to pick your pocket.<br />
  She realized her wallet was gone after someone picked her pocket on the subway.<br />
  The police caught the man trying to pick someone's pocket at the train station.<br />
  He lost his phone when someone picked his pocket during the concert.
</div>
