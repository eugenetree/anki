# Flash Card: Burn Down

## Front

Стара ферма <u>згоріла дотла</u> під час нічної пожежі.
Палій намагався <u>спалити дотла</u> покинутий склад.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The old farm burned down during the night fire. The arsonist tried to burn down the abandoned warehouse."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: burn down<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: (1) to be destroyed by fire; (2) to destroy a building or place by setting it on fire<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Their house burned down while they were away.<br />
  The fire spread quickly and burned down the entire block.<br />
  Someone tried to burn down the old school building.<br />
  The restaurant burned down after a kitchen accident.
</div>
