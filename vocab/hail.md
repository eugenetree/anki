# Flash Card: Hail

## Front

Вчора під час грози випав сильний <u>град</u>.
Коли почався <u>град</u>, <u>градини</u> були розміром з горіх.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "Yesterday during the thunderstorm, heavy hail fell. When the hail started, the hailstones were the size of a walnut."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: hail<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: small balls of ice that fall from the sky like rain<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The hail damaged many cars in the parking lot.<br />
  We had to take shelter when it started to hail.<br />
  The hailstones were as big as golf balls.
</div>
