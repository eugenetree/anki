# Flash Card: Fly off the handle

## Front

Коли я випадково розбив його улюблену чашку, він одразу <u>втратив самовладання</u>.
Вона <u>різко розлютилася</u> через таку дрібницю.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "When I accidentally broke his favorite cup, he immediately flew off the handle." / "She flew off the handle over such a small thing."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: fly off the handle<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to suddenly become very angry and lose control of your emotions, often in an unreasonable way<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  There's no need to fly off the handle just because I'm a few minutes late.<br />
  My boss flew off the handle when he saw the mistake in the report.<br />
  Try to stay calm and don't fly off the handle during the meeting.<br />
  He flies off the handle at the smallest criticism.
</div>
