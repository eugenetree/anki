# Flash Card: tremendous

## Front (Ukrainian)

Він зробив <u>колосальний</u> прогрес у вивченні англійської мови.

## Back (English)

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He made tremendous progress in learning English."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: tremendous<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: extremely large (physically or in amount), great, or impressive; very good or excellent<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The concert was a tremendous success with thousands of people attending.<br />
  She has tremendous talent for playing the piano.<br />
  We had a tremendous amount of work to do before the deadline.
</div>
