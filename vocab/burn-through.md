# Flash Card: Burn Through

## Front

Він <u>швидко витратив</u> всі свої заощадження за місяць.
Ми <u>витратили</u> всі запаси води за перший день походу.
(like use, but use something very quickly, quicker than it was supposed to happen)

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He burned through all his savings in a month. We burned through all the water supplies on the first day of the hike."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: burn through<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to use up or consume something quickly, especially money, resources, or supplies<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  She burned through her inheritance in just two years.<br />
  The company burned through millions of dollars before going bankrupt.<br />
  We burned through all our food supplies during the camping trip.<br />
  He burned through his vacation days by taking long weekends.
</div>
