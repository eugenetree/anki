# Flash Card: Oversleep

## Front

Я <u>проспав</u> і запізнився на роботу.
Вона встановила три будильники, щоб не <u>проспати</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "I overslept and was late for work. She set three alarms so she wouldn't oversleep."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: oversleep<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to sleep longer than intended or planned; to miss waking up at the proper time<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I overslept this morning and missed my bus.<br />
  He overslept and was late for the meeting.<br />
  I'm afraid I might oversleep tomorrow.<br />
  She overslept because her alarm didn't go off.
</div>
