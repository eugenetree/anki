# Flash Card: Blow Off Steam

## Front

Після важкого тижня мені потрібно <u>випустити пар</u> у спортзалі.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "After a hard week, I need to blow off steam at the gym."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: blow off steam<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to release or get rid of strong emotions, especially anger or stress, through physical activity or other means<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I like to go for a run to blow off steam after work.<br />
  He plays basketball on weekends to blow off some steam.<br />
  She blew off steam by talking to her best friend about her problems.<br />
  After that intense meeting, we all needed to blow off steam.
</div>
