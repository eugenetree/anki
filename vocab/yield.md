# Flash Card: Yield

## Front

Ця інвестиція <u>приносить</u> хороші прибутки щороку.
Це поле <u>дає</u> високий урожай пшениці.
<u>Дохідність</u> цієї облігації становить 5 відсотків на рік.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "This investment yields good returns every year. This field yields a high wheat harvest. The yield on this bond is 5 percent per year."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: yield<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: (verb) to produce or provide something, especially as a result of effort or investment; (noun) the amount produced or the return on an investment<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The new farming methods yielded better crops this season.<br />
  His research finally yielded important results after years of work.<br />
  The bond has a yield of 5% per year.<br />
  The crop yield was lower than expected due to the drought.<br />
  This investment strategy yields consistent returns over time.
</div>
