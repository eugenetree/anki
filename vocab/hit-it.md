# Flash Card: Hit It

## Front

На вчорашній вечірці було мало людей, тож ми вирішили <u>піти звідти</u>. (not leave)

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "There were few people at yesterday's party, so we decided to hit it."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: hit it (not leave)<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: (informal) to leave or depart from a place; a casual way to suggest going or getting out of somewhere<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  This party is boring, let's hit it.<br />
  We should hit it soon if we want to avoid traffic.<br />
  The concert's over, time to hit it.
</div>
