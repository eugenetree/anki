# Flash Card: Steer Away

## Front

Він намагався <u>відвести/u> від цієї теми під час розмови. (figurative)
Водій швидко <u>відвернув</u> машину, щоб уникнути зіткнення. (literal)

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He tried to steer away from this topic during the conversation. The driver quickly steered away to avoid a collision."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: steer away<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to avoid something or someone; to change direction to avoid a collision or problem; to avoid discussing a particular topic<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  We need to steer away from controversial topics during the meeting.<br />
  The captain steered the ship away from the storm.<br />
  She always steers away from discussing her personal life.<br />
  The cyclist steered away from the pothole just in time.
</div>
