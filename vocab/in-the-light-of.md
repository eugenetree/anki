# Flash Card: In the Light Of

## Front

<u>У світлі</u> нових фактів, ми повинні переглянути наш план.
<u>У світлі</u> останніх подій, він змінив свою думку.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "In the light of new facts, we need to revise our plan. In the light of recent events, he changed his mind."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: in the light of<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: considering or taking into account; used when reconsidering something based on new information, circumstances, or evidence<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  In the light of recent developments, we decided to postpone the meeting.<br />
  The decision was made in the light of new evidence that came to light.<br />
  In the light of what happened, I think we should reconsider our approach.<br />
  The company changed its policy in the light of customer feedback.
</div>
