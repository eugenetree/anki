# Flash Card: Owe

## Front

Я <u>винен</u> тобі 50 доларів за ту вечерю в ресторані.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "I owe you 50 dollars for that dinner at the restaurant."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: owe<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to have a debt to someone; to be obligated to pay or give something to someone, typically money, gratitude, or an explanation<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  How much do I owe you for the groceries?<br />
  She owes me an apology for what she said.<br />
  I owe my success to my parents' support.
</div>
