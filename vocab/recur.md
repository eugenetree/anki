# Flash Card: Recur

## Front

Ця проблема постійно <u>повторюється</u> щомісяця, і ми не можемо знайти рішення.
Ми не можемо бути впевнені, що це не <u>повториться</u> знову.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "This problem keeps recurring every month, and we can't find a solution. We can't be certain it won't recur again."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: recur<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to happen again or repeatedly, especially something unpleasant or unwanted that comes back after a period of time<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The headaches recurred several times a week.<br />
  The same issue recurs in every project we work on.<br />
  If the symptoms recur, please contact your doctor immediately.<br />
  We can't be certain it won't recur.
</div>
