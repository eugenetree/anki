# Flash Card: Vague

## Front

Його пояснення було надто <u>розпливчастим</u>, я нічого не зрозумів.
Вона дала <u>неясну</u> відповідь на моє запитання.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "His explanation was too vague, I didn't understand anything." / "She gave a vague answer to my question."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: vague<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: not clear, precise, or definite in meaning or expression; unclear or uncertain<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  His instructions were too vague, so I didn't know what to do.<br />
  She has only a vague memory of what happened that night.<br />
  The plan is still quite vague and needs more details.<br />
  He gave me a vague answer about when he would finish the project.
</div>
