# Flash Card: Ignition

## Front

Він вставив ключ у <u>замок запалювання</u> і завів машину.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He put the key in the ignition and started the car."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: ignition<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: the system in a vehicle (typically using a key or button) that starts the engine; also refers to the process of catching fire or beginning to burn<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Turn the key in the ignition to start the car.<br />
  The ignition system was faulty, so the car wouldn't start.<br />
  She pressed the ignition button and the engine roared to life.<br />
  The ignition of the fuel caused a massive explosion.
</div>
