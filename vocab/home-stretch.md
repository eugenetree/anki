# Flash Card: Home Stretch

## Front

Ми майже закінчили проект, залишилася тільки <u>фінальна частина</u>.
Тримайся! Ти вже на <u>фінальній прямій</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "We've almost finished the project, only the home stretch remains." / "Hang tough! You're already on the home stretch."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: home stretch<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: the final part or stage of something, especially a long process or journey; originally from horse racing, referring to the last straight part of the track before the finish line<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  We're in the home stretch now, just two more weeks until the wedding.<br />
  The team is on the home stretch of the championship season.<br />
  After months of preparation, we've finally reached the home stretch of our exam period.<br />
  Hang tough. You're in the home stretch.
</div>
