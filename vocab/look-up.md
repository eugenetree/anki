# Flash Card: Look up

## Front

Я не пам'ятаю його номер телефону, мені потрібно <u>знайти його</u> в інтернеті.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "I don't remember his phone number, I need to look it up on the internet."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: look up<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to search for information in a reference source such as a dictionary, book, or online<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I need to look up the address before we go.<br />
  She looked up the recipe on the internet.<br />
  You can look up any word you don't understand in this dictionary.
</div>
