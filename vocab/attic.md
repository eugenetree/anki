# Flash Card: Attic

## Front

Ми зберігаємо старі речі на <u>горищі</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "We store old things in the attic."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: attic<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: a space or room just below the roof of a building, typically used for storage<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I found some old photos in the attic.<br />
  The attic was full of dust and cobwebs.<br />
  They converted the attic into a cozy bedroom.
