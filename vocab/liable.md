# Flash Card: Liable

## Front

Він <u>схильний</u> забути про зустріч, якщо його не нагадати.
Якщо ти так скажеш їй, вона <u>цілком може</u> образитися.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He's liable to forget the meeting if you don't remind him. If you say that to her, she's liable to get offended."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: liable to<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: likely to do something, especially something undesirable or risky<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  He's liable to lose his temper if you push him too far.<br />
  If you leave food out, it's liable to attract insects.<br />
  She's liable to say something she'll regret when she's angry.
</div>
