# Flash Card: Make Hay While the Sun Shines

## Front

Зараз у тебе є чудова можливість, тому <u>скористайся моментом, поки він є</u>. (idiom like куй железо пока гарячо)

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "You have a great opportunity now, so make hay while the sun shines."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: make hay while the sun shines<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: an idiom meaning to take advantage of favorable conditions or opportunities while they last; to act while circumstances are good because they may not last long. Similar to "strike while the iron is hot."<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The weather is perfect today, so let's make hay while the sun shines and finish the outdoor work.<br />
  Business is booming right now—we need to make hay while the sun shines.<br />
  She's making hay while the sun shines by working extra hours during the busy season.<br />
  The stock market is up, so investors are making hay while the sun shines.
</div>
