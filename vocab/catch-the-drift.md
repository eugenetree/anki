# Flash Card: Catch the drift

## Front

Він не пояснив усі деталі, але я <u>зрозумів загальну суть</u>. (idiom)

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He didn't explain all the details, but I caught the drift."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: catch the drift (also: get the drift)<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to understand the general meaning or main point of what someone is saying, even without all the details<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I didn't understand every word, but I caught the drift of his speech.<br />
  She was hinting at something, and I think I get the drift.<br />
  Do you catch my drift, or should I explain more clearly?<br />
  He spoke quickly, but I caught the drift of what he was trying to say.
</div>
