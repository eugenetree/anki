# Flash Card: Nod

## Front

Коли я запитав, чи він згоден, він просто <u>кивнув</u> головою.
Вона <u>кивнула</u>, щоб показати, що розуміє.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "When I asked if he agreed, he just nodded his head. She nodded to show that she understood."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: nod<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to move your head up and down to indicate agreement, understanding, or greeting<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  He nodded in agreement when I explained the plan.<br />
  She nodded her head to say yes without speaking.<br />
  The teacher nodded approvingly at the student's answer.
</div>
