# Flash Card: something got into him

## Front (Ukrainian)

Щось <u>на нього найшло</u> сьогодні - він поводиться дуже дивно.

## Back (English)

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "Something got into him today - he's behaving very strangely."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: something got into him<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: an expression meaning someone is acting unusually, strangely, or out of character<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Something got into him at the party - he was dancing on tables.<br />
  I don't know what got into him, but he's been working late every night this week.<br />
  Something must have gotten into him because he's never this talkative.
</div>
