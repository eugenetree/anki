# Flash Card: Fog Up

## Front

Вікна в машині <u>запотіли</u> через холодну погоду.
Мої окуляри <u>запотіли</u>, коли я увійшов у теплу кімнату з вулиці.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The car windows fogged up because of the cold weather. My glasses fogged up when I entered the warm room from outside."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: fog up<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to become covered with condensation or mist, making something unclear or difficult to see through (usually referring to glass, windows, mirrors, or glasses)<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The bathroom mirror fogged up after I took a hot shower.<br />
  My car windows always fog up in the winter when I turn on the heat.<br />
  His glasses fogged up when he walked into the warm building from the cold outside.
</div>
