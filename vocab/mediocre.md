# Flash Card: Mediocre

## Front

Фільм був <u>посереднім</u> — не жахливим, але й нічого особливого.
Його робота виявилася <u>посередньою</u>, і клієнт був розчарований.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The movie was mediocre — not terrible, but nothing special. His work turned out to be mediocre, and the client was disappointed."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: mediocre<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: of only average quality; not good enough to be impressive or satisfying<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The restaurant got mediocre reviews online.<br />
  She didn't want to settle for a mediocre performance — she aimed for excellence.<br />
  The team had a mediocre season, finishing in the middle of the standings.
</div>
