# Flash Card: Hunch

## Front

У мене була <u>здогадка</u>, що зустріч перенесуть на п'ятницю.
Вона не мала доказів, але її <u>інтуїтивне відчуття</u> виявилося правильним.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "I had a hunch that the meeting would be moved to Friday. She had no proof, but her hunch turned out to be right."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: hunch<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: a hunch is a specific guess that something is true or will happen, even without clear proof. A feeling is broader and can be emotional or physical; intuition is the general ability to understand something without reasoning; a hunch is one particular intuitive guess.<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I had a hunch you would call today.<br />
  Her hunch about the missing files was correct.<br />
  We followed his hunch and found the problem quickly.
</div>
