# Flash Card: Check In

## Front

Я <u>зв'яжуся з тобою</u> пізніше, щоб повідомити про прогрес.
Давай <u>на зв'язок завтра</u>, обговоримо, як у тебе справи з проектом.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "I'll check in with you later to update you on the progress." / "Let's check in tomorrow, we'll discuss how things are going with your project."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: check in (with someone)<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to contact someone to give them information about what you are doing or to see how they are doing; to communicate or make contact to provide an update or see if everything is okay<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I'll check in with you next week to see how the project is going.<br />
  Make sure to check in with your team regularly during remote work.<br />
  She checks in with her elderly neighbor every day to make sure she's okay.<br />
  Let me check in with my manager and get back to you.
</div>
