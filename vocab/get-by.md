# Flash Card: Get By

## Front

Нам вистачить цих грошей, щоб <u>прожити</u> до кінця місяця.
Ми не багаті, але якось <u>обходимося</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "We'll have enough money to get by until the end of the month. We're not rich, but we get by somehow."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: get by<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to manage to live or do something with difficulty, especially with limited money or resources; to cope or survive in a situation<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  We don't have much money, but we get by.<br />
  She gets by with part-time work and some help from her family.<br />
  They can barely get by on one income.<br />
  I know enough Spanish to get by when I travel.
</div>
