# Flash Card: Look out for

## Front

Мій старший брат завжди <u>піклується про мене</u> і допомагає, коли мені важко.
У цьому бізнесі ти повинен <u>дбати про себе</u>, бо ніхто інший цього не зробить.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "My older brother always looks out for me and helps when things are tough for me." / "In this business, you need to look out for yourself because nobody else will do it."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: look out for (someone)<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to take care of someone's interests, safety, or well-being; to protect someone<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I'll look out for you while you're away from home.<br />
  She always looks out for her younger sister at school.<br />
  You need to look out for yourself - nobody else will.<br />
  My best friend always looks out for me when I'm in trouble.
</div>
