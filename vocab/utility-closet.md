# Flash Card: Utility Closet

## Front

Всі мітли, швабри та миючі засоби зберігаються у <u>підсобці</u>.
Пральна машина та сушарка знаходяться у <u>підсобці</u> поруч з кухнею.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "All brooms, mops, and cleaning supplies are stored in the utility closet.
  The washing machine and dryer are in the utility room next to the kitchen."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: utility closet / utility room<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: a space for storing cleaning supplies, tools, and equipment. A utility closet is a small closet-sized space in commercial buildings (schools, offices, hotels). A utility room is a larger room, typically in homes, that may contain a washing machine, dryer, water heater, and storage<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The janitor keeps all the cleaning equipment in the utility closet.<br />
  Can you grab some paper towels from the utility closet?<br />
  We installed a new washing machine in the utility room.<br />
  The utility room is next to the kitchen and has plenty of storage space.
</div>
