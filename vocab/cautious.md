# Flash Card: Cautious

## Front

Вона завжди <u>обережна</u> з незнайомцями і не довіряє їм одразу.
Будь <u>обережний</u>, коли йдеш вночі сам.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "She is always cautious with strangers and doesn't trust them right away. Be cautious when walking alone at night."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: cautious<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: careful to avoid potential problems or dangers; acting with care and attention to avoid risks<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  You should be cautious when crossing busy streets.<br />
  She gave a cautious response to the question, not wanting to commit.<br />
  The company took a cautious approach to expanding into new markets.<br />
  He's naturally cautious about making big decisions.
