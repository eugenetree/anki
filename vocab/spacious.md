# Flash Card: Spacious

## Front

Їхня нова квартира дуже <u>простора</u> і світла.
Мені подобаються <u>просторі</u> кімнати з високими стелями.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "Their new apartment is very spacious and bright." / "I like spacious rooms with high ceilings."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: spacious<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: having a lot of space inside; large and roomy with plenty of room to move around<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The hotel room was surprisingly spacious for the price.<br />
  They're looking for a more spacious house for their growing family.<br />
  The restaurant has a spacious dining area that can seat 100 guests.
</div>
