# Flash Card: hang in

## Front (Ukrainian)

Коли справи йдуть погано, важливо <u>триматися</u> і не втрачати надію.

## Back (English)

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "When things go wrong, it's important to hang in and not lose hope."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: hang in<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to persevere, to continue trying despite difficulties, to not give up<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Hang in there, the exam will be over soon.<br />
  Even though the project is challenging, we need to hang in and finish it.<br />
  She told me to hang in when I was feeling discouraged about learning English.
</div>
