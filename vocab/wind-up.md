# Flash Card: Wind up

## Front

Якщо ти продовжиш так поводитися, ти <u>опинишся</u> у великих неприємностях.
Якщо я залишу їх вдома, я просто <u>зрештою з'їм</u> їх усі сам.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "If you keep behaving like this, you'll wind up in big trouble." / "If I leave them at home, I'll just wind up eating them all myself."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: wind up<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to find yourself in a particular situation or place, especially one that you did not plan or expect<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  If you don't study, you'll wind up failing the exam.<br />
  We took a wrong turn and wound up in a different city.<br />
  He wound up working in the same company as his father.<br />
  Keep spending like that and you'll wind up broke.<br />
  If I leave them at home, I'll just wind up eating them all myself.
</div>
