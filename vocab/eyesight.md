# Flash Card: Eyesight

## Front

Після багатьох годин за комп'ютером мій <u>зір</u> став гіршим.
У дідуся досі дуже добрий <u>зір</u>, тому він читає без окулярів.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "After many hours at the computer, my eyesight got worse. Grandpa still has very good eyesight, so he reads without glasses."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: eyesight<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: the ability to see, especially how well or badly someone can see<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  My eyesight has improved since I started wearing the right glasses.<br />
  She has excellent eyesight and can read tiny text from far away.<br />
  Too much screen time can make your eyesight feel tired.
