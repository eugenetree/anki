# Flash Card: Turn In

## Front

Він <u>здав</u> свого партнера поліції.
Вона вирішила <u>повідомити</u> про злочинця владі.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He turned in his partner to the police. She decided to turn in the criminal to the authorities."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: turn in (someone)<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to report someone to the police or authorities, usually because they have done something wrong or illegal; to inform on someone<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The witness decided to turn in the suspect.<br />
  He turned himself in to the police after the robbery.<br />
  She was afraid to turn in her friend.<br />
  They offered a reward for anyone who turns in the fugitive.
</div>
