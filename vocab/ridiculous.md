# Flash Card: Ridiculous

## Front

Він запропонував таку <u>абсурдну</u> ціну, що я відразу відмовився.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He proposed such a ridiculous price that I immediately refused."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: ridiculous<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: extremely silly, unreasonable, or absurd; deserving to be laughed at<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The idea of walking 50 miles in one day seems ridiculous.<br />
  She was wearing a ridiculous hat with feathers and bells.<br />
  It's ridiculous to wait three hours just to buy a coffee.<br />
  His excuse for being late was absolutely ridiculous.
</div>
