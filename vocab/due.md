# Flash Card: Due

## Front

Звіт <u>має бути готовий</u> до завтра.
Коли твоя домашня робота <u>має бути здана</u>?

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The report is due tomorrow. When is your homework due?"<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: due<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: expected or scheduled to happen, arrive, or be completed at a particular time<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The project is due next Friday.<br />
  When is the baby due?<br />
  My library books are due back tomorrow.<br />
  The payment is due by the end of the month.
