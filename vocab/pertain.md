# Flash Card: Pertain

## Front

Це питання <u>стосується</u> нашого проекту. (formal, not "relate")
Ця інформація <u>стосується</u> лише членів команди. (formal, not "relate")

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "This question pertains to our project. This information pertains only to team members."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: pertain<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to relate to, be connected with, or be relevant to something<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  These rules pertain to all employees in the company.<br />
  The documents pertain to the legal case from last year.<br />
  Please read the section that pertains to your department.
</div>
