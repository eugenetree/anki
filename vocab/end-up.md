# Flash Card: End Up

## Front

Ми планували піти в парк, але <u>опинилися</u> в кафе через дощ.
Як ти тут <u>опинився</u>?

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "We planned to go to the park, but ended up in a café because of the rain. How did you end up here?"<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: end up<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to eventually reach a particular state, place, or situation, especially after a series of events or changes, often unexpectedly<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I thought I would study medicine, but I ended up studying law instead.<br />
  We got lost and ended up in a completely different neighborhood.<br />
  If you keep spending money like that, you'll end up broke.<br />
  She started as an intern but ended up becoming the company's CEO.
</div>
