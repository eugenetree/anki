# Flash Card: Back Off

## Front

Він наближався до мене агресивно, тому я сказав йому <u>відступити</u>.
Вона продовжувала наполягати, поки я не попросив її <u>відчепитися</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He was approaching me aggressively, so I told him to back off. She kept insisting, until I asked her to back off."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: back off<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to move away from someone or something, or to stop pressuring, bothering, or being aggressive toward someone<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The security guard told the crowd to back off.<br />
  You need to back off and give her some space.<br />
  He finally backed off when he realized I wasn't interested.<br />
  The police ordered the protesters to back off from the building.
</div>
