# Flash Card: Break Even

## Front

Наш бізнес нарешті <u>вийшов в нуль</u> після першого року роботи.
Ми сподіваємося <u>вийти в нуль</u> до кінця місяця.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "Our business finally broke even after the first year of operation. We hope to break even by the end of the month."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: break even<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to reach a point where income equals expenses, resulting in neither profit nor loss<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The company expects to break even by the end of the year.<br />
  After months of losses, we finally broke even last quarter.<br />
  It took us two years to break even on our initial investment.
</div>
