# Flash Card: Cut Out

## Front

Перестань кричати! <u>Припини це</u> зараз же!
<u>Годі вже з</u> цими фантазіями. / Досить вигадувати.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "Stop shouting! Cut it out right now! Cut it out with the fantasy already."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: cut out / cut it out<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to stop doing something, especially something annoying or inappropriate; used as an imperative to tell someone to stop their behavior<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Cut it out! You're being too loud.<br />
  I told him to cut out the nonsense and focus on his work.<br />
  She needs to cut out complaining and start taking action.
</div>
