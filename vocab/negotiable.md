# Flash Card: Negotiable

## Front

Ціна на цей автомобіль <u>обговорюється</u>.
Назви одну річ у цьому світі, яка <u>не підлягає обговоренню</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The price for this car is negotiable. Name one thing in this world that is not negotiable."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: negotiable<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: open to discussion or modification; able to be negotiated or bargained<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The salary is negotiable depending on your experience.<br />
  All prices are negotiable, so feel free to make an offer.<br />
  The deadline for this project is negotiable if you need more time.<br />
  The terms of the agreement are still negotiable.
</div>
