# Flash Card: Spouse

## Front

На цю зустріч можна прийти зі своїм <u>чоловіком або дружиною</u>.
У заяві потрібно вказати ім'я вашого <u>чоловіка або дружини</u>.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "You can come to this meeting with your spouse. You need to write your spouse's name on the form."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: spouse<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: a person's husband or wife; partner is broader and may mean someone you are not married to<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Employees may bring their spouse to the company dinner.<br />
  Please include your spouse's contact information on the form.<br />
  Her spouse works at the same hospital.
</div>
