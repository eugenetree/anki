# Flash Card: Catch One's Breath

## Front

Після підйому сходами мені треба хвилинку, щоб <u>перевести подих</u>.
Зачекай біля входу, я хочу <u>віддихатися</u> після пробіжки.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "After climbing the stairs, I need a minute to catch my breath. Wait by the entrance; I want to catch my breath after the run."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: catch one's breath<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to rest briefly until you can breathe normally again after physical effort<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I had to stop halfway up the hill to catch my breath.<br />
  Give me a second to catch my breath before we keep walking.<br />
  She leaned against the wall and caught her breath after the race.
</div>
