# Flash Card: Turn Down

## Front

Вона <u>відхилила</u> пропозицію про роботу, бо зарплата була занадто низькою.
Він <u>відмовився</u> від запрошення на вечірку, бо був зайнятий.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "She turned down the job offer because the salary was too low. He turned down the invitation to the party because he was busy."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: turn down<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: (1) to reject or refuse something, such as an offer, invitation, or request; (2) to reduce the volume, intensity, or level of something, especially sound or heat<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  He turned down the invitation to the party.<br />
  I had to turn down three job offers before finding the right one.<br />
  Can you turn down the heat? It's getting too warm in here.<br />
  She turned down the volume on her phone during the meeting.
</div>
