# Flash Card: Mobster

## Front

Поліція заарештувала <u>члена мафії</u> після довгого розслідування.
У фільмі старий <u>мафіозі</u> контролював кілька незаконних бізнесів.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "The police arrested a mobster after a long investigation. In the movie, an old mobster controlled several illegal businesses."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: mobster<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: a criminal who belongs to a mob or organized crime group, especially one connected with the Mafia; a mobster is usually tied to organized crime, while a gangster is broader and can mean any member of a criminal gang<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The mobster was accused of running an illegal gambling business.<br />
  Several mobsters met in secret to discuss the deal.<br />
  The movie tells the story of a young man who becomes a powerful mobster.
</div>
