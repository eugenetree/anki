# Flash Card: Driveway

## Front

Вона гралася на <u>під'їзній дорозі</u> перед будинком.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "She was playing on the driveway in front of the house."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: driveway<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: a private road leading from a public road to a house, garage, or other building, typically used for vehicles<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Please don't block the driveway with your car.<br />
  The snow on the driveway needs to be shoveled before we can leave.<br />
  He pulled into the driveway and turned off the engine.
</div>
