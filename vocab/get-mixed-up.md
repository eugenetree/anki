# Flash Card: Get Mixed Up

## Front

Він <u>зв'язався</u> з поганою компанією в університеті.
Твій племінник <u>зв'язався</u> з досить неприємними типами.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He got mixed up with the wrong crowd at university. Your nephew got mixed up with some pretty nasty customers."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: get mixed up (with someone/something)<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to become involved with someone or something, especially people or activities that are bad or cause trouble; to start associating with the wrong people or situations<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  She got mixed up with a group of criminals and ended up in jail.<br />
  I don't want my son to get mixed up with drugs.<br />
  He got mixed up in illegal activities when he was younger.<br />
  Try not to get mixed up with that crowd—they're nothing but trouble.
</div>
