# Flash Card: Blackmail

## Front

Він намагався <u>шантажувати</u> мене, погрожуючи розповісти всім мій секрет.
Це був справжній <u>шантаж</u>, і я вирішив звернутися до поліції.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He tried to blackmail me, threatening to tell everyone my secret. It was real blackmail, and I decided to go to the police."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: blackmail<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: (verb) to demand money or something else from someone by threatening to reveal embarrassing or damaging information about them; (noun) the act or crime of demanding money or something else by threatening to reveal damaging information<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  He was arrested for attempting to blackmail his former employer.<br />
  The politician resigned after being accused of blackmail.<br />
  She refused to give in to his blackmail attempts.<br />
  They reported the blackmail to the authorities immediately.
</div>
