# Flash Card: Take Over

## Front

Коли менеджер пішов у відпустку, мені довелося <u>взяти на себе</u> його обов'язки.
Я зроблю першу частину проекту, а потім ти <u>візьмеш на себе</u> решту.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "When the manager went on vacation, I had to take over his responsibilities."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: take over<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to assume control, responsibility, or ownership of something, often replacing someone else<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  She will take over the team when the current leader retires.<br />
  Can you take over this project for me next week?<br />
  He took over the family business after his father passed away.<br />
  I'll handle the first part of the project, and that's where you will take over.
</div>
