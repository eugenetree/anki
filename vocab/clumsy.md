# Flash Card: Clumsy

## Front

Він такий <u>незграбний</u>, що постійно щось ронить або розбиває.

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He's so clumsy that he constantly drops or breaks something."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: clumsy<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: awkward in movement or handling things, often accidentally dropping or breaking things<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  I'm so clumsy, I just knocked over my coffee cup.<br />
  She felt clumsy in her new high heels.<br />
  His clumsy attempt to fix the computer made things worse.<br />
  The waiter was a bit clumsy and spilled some water on the table.
</div>
