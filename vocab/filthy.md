# Flash Card: Filthy

## Front

Ця ванна кімната така <u>огидна</u>, що я не можу нею користуватися. (literal meaning)
Його кімната завжди <u>брудна</u> - там повно сміття та пилу. (literal meaning)
Він використовував <u>непристойну</u> мову на публіці, що мене шокувало. (figurative meaning)
Його <u>мерзенні</u> думки про жінок були просто огидні. (figurative meaning)

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "This bathroom is so filthy that I can't use it. His room is always filthy - it's full of garbage and dust. He used filthy language in public, which shocked me. His filthy thoughts about women were simply disgusting."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: filthy<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: (1) extremely dirty, disgusting, or covered with dirt (literal); (2) morally offensive, obscene, or indecent (figurative); (3) in "filthy rich" - extremely wealthy (idiomatic)<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  The kitchen was filthy after the party - dishes everywhere and food on the floor.<br />
  He refused to wear the filthy clothes that had been on the floor for weeks.<br />
  The streets were filthy with litter and garbage after the festival.<br />
  She was shocked by the filthy language he used in front of children.<br />
  I can't believe he has such filthy thoughts about his colleagues.<br />
  He inherited millions and is now filthy rich.
</div>
