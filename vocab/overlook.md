# Overlook

## Front

Я <u>пропустив</u> важливу деталь в документі. (not miss, not didn't notice)

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "I overlooked an important detail in the
  document."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: overlook<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to fail to notice or consider something<br /><br />
  <strong>Difference</strong>:<br />
  • <strong>overlook</strong> - formal, implies failing to notice something that
  should have been noticed<br />
  • <strong>miss</strong> - more general, casual; can mean many things (miss a
  person, miss a target, etc.)<br />
  • <strong>didn't notice</strong> - simple past phrase, neutral; just describes
  the fact of not noticing<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  It's easy to overlook small mistakes when you're tired.<br />
  The teacher overlooked several errors in my essay.<br />
  Don't overlook the fine print in the contract.
</div>
