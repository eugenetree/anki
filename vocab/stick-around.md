# Flash Card: Stick Around

## Front

Можеш <u>залишитися</u> ще трохи? Я скоро повернуся.
Він вирішив <u>залишитися</u> до кінця концерту.
(informal synonym of "stay")

## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "Can you stick around a bit longer? I'll be back soon. He decided to stick around until the end of the concert."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: stick around<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: to stay in a place, especially waiting for something or someone; to remain somewhere instead of leaving<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  Can you stick around for a few more minutes?<br />
  I'll stick around until you finish your work.<br />
  He decided to stick around and see what happens next.
</div>
