## Back

<div style="text-align: left;">
  <strong>Translation</strong>: "He was very eager to start the new project. The children were eager to see the presents."<br /><br />
  <strong>Word</strong>: eager<br /><br />
  <strong>Explanation</strong>: having or showing keen interest, intense desire, or impatient expectancy; wanting very much to do or have something<br /><br />
  <strong>Examples</strong>:<br />
  She was eager to learn more about the new technology.<br />
  The students were eager to start their summer vacation.<br />
  He's always eager to help others when they need it.<br />
  I'm eager to hear what you think about the proposal.
